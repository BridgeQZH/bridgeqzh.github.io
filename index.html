Zihan Qiao

Robotics, control, autonomous systems, and AI engineer with a strong interest in building practical systems that connect research ideas to real-world deployment.

I studied Systems, Control and Robotics at KTH Royal Institute of Technology and earned my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering (Automation) from Zhejiang University. My background combines robotics, perception, planning, reinforcement learning, and production-focused engineering.

Across automotive and autonomous systems work, I have built and tuned control models, debugged system-level integration issues, developed real-time ROS perception and navigation pipelines, and shipped projects spanning drone autonomy, adaptive traffic signal control, and full-stack web applications.

I enjoy work that sits at the boundary of intelligent systems and usable products: robust enough for reality, but still ambitious enough to be exciting. Outside engineering, I also care about bridge, magic, film, and creative online content.
